A Brief About Me

Tattooer, Fine Artist, Professional Foodie

An Austin, TX native - I took on artistic values at an early age when introduced by my father. As I gre, I explored many mediums from paper and pencil to graphic design and more. I found my love in tattooing early and at the age of 18 moved into downtown Austin to pursue my passion. Not long after I was welcomed into Shaman Body Modifications by my now good friends Pinnapple and Justin Vickers.

Flash foward to today, at 24 and over 5 years working alongside Justin, I have been able to push myself in a very comfortable direction with my art and abilities. I really enjoy illustrative design, using aspects of realism to bring my sometimes playful style an interesting dynamic. I have fun with just about anything presented to me and am always looking to my clients to help push myself and obtain a higher quality piece that can be enjoyed in the years to come. I love working with color and try to show this in what I do which can be seen in the galleries.

I am Appointment only so please contact me directly, and with some advanced notice so that I may get you into the calendar accordingly.
